🌱 About Ayuva

Bridging the gap between modern nutrition science and ancient Ayurvedic wisdom for holistic healthcare

The Problem We Solve

Currently, Ayurvedic diet charts are still written manually by practitioners. Existing software solutions focus primarily on macro and micro nutrients but completely ignore Ayurvedic nutritional concepts like Rasa (six tastes), Virya (heating/cooling properties), and food compatibility based on individual constitution.

This creates inefficiencies, reduces accuracy, and makes it harder for practitioners to deliver truly holistic dietary care rooted in Ayurvedic principles.

Our Solution

Ayuva is a comprehensive cloud-based platform that seamlessly integrates modern nutritional analysis with traditional Ayurvedic dietary wisdom. Our AI-powered system considers both scientific nutrition data and ancient principles to create personalized, effective diet plans.

We make it possible for practitioners to generate Ayurveda-compliant diet charts in minutes instead of hours, while ensuring nutritional accuracy and cultural authenticity.

Technology Meets Tradition

  • 8000+ food database with dual analysis
  • AI algorithms trained on Ayurvedic principles
  • Modern UX with traditional wisdom
  • Cloud-based for accessibility
  • Mobile-first design for modern practitioners

Impact & Vision

Our vision is to empower Ayurvedic practitioners worldwide with tools that honor traditional knowledge while leveraging modern technology. We aim to make personalized, constitution-based nutrition accessible to everyone.

By digitizing and standardizing Ayurvedic dietary practices, we're helping preserve and propagate this ancient science for future generations.
